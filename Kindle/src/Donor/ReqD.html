Full Description:

A first aid kit is a portable collection of medical supplies intended for basic treatment of minor injuries and illnesses. It typically contains adhesive bandages, antiseptic wipes, ointments, gauze pads, and rolls, along with some non-prescription pain medication. These kits are not meant for serious medical emergencies, but rather for cuts, scrapes, burns, sprains, and other bumps and bruises. They are considered a passive medical device, since they are used for basic care and not for operating or diagnosing.
